● APPROACH

I work with Freudian Psychoanalysis, a deep approach that seeks to understand the unconscious processes that influence our thoughts, emotions and behaviors.

Main pillars of Psychoanalysis:

  • Exploration of the unconscious and dreams
  • Analysis of transference and the therapeutic relationship
  • Investigation of childhood experiences
  • Understanding defense mechanisms
  • Work with free associations

Fundamental concepts I work with: Unconscious, repression, drives, Oedipus complex, and the topographical and structural models of the mind (Id, Ego and Superego).

Areas of expertise: Neuroses, anguish, emotional conflicts, relationship difficulties, existential crises and self-knowledge processes.
